About Our School

Faizan Public School and Degree College has been a cornerstone of academic excellence in Besham, Shangla since 1991. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing and disciplined environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and ethically. By blending modern teaching methodologies with strong traditional values, we prepare our students to become confident leaders and responsible citizens of tomorrow.
